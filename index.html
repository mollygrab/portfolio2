About me

Originally from Lancaster, Pennsylvania, I'm currently a junior at Saint Joseph's University in Philadelphia. I major in both English and Communications Studies, and I'm interested in web content development, digital content strategy, digital media management, and crafting effective writing.

My coursework and internship experiences thus far have cultivated my writing, research, presentation, and critical thinking skills. They have also opened my eyes to importance of effective communication. Individuals, organizations, and businesses cannot be successful without the power of persuasive words, and as someone who is fascinated by words, I want to challenge myself to craft impactful writing in my career.

On this site, you'll find a portfolio that includes writing samples and more information about my work and interests, my blog, and my résumé. Feel free to contact me via email or social media.
