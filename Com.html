Creative Work

As a Communications Studies major at St. Joe's, I have produced a number of more creative works. Below you'll find examples such as a video and a sound project. On the side of this page, you can click to view the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript coding work I've completed on GitHub.

Sample 1: Food project video

This video was made in order to implement logos, ethos, and pathos into the presentaion of food. In making the video, I explored the various ways in which an audience can make meaning.

Project 3: The Making of an Easter Tradition from Molly Grab on Vimeo.

Sample 2: Sound project

In this project, I was tasked with creating and recording my own origninal sounds and presenting them in a soundscape. I chose to sample sounds from my gym -- weights clanging, shoes thumping, and machines beeping. I edited the sounds and put them together in the following soundscape. The project forced me to realize that sound plays a crucial role in the sensory experience of an audience.
