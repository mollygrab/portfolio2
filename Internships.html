Internship experience

In addition to my internship postions at The Hawk Newspaper, I have completed two internships and have been hired to complete another this summer.

Internet Marketing Intern, ProspectMX

In the summer of 2015, I worked as an intern for ProspectMX, a marketing company based in Lancaster, Pennsylvania. I worked directly with Prospect's Chief Copywriter in order to write and develop web content for various clients. This content included blogs, web pages, fact sheets, infographics, social media style guides, and BuzzFeed articles/quizzes. I also assisted with keyword research learned the basics of SEO practices.

Research and Archives Intern, AACA Museum

In the summer of 2015 I also worked as an intern for the Antique Automobile Club of America Museum in Hershey, Pennsylvania. While cars aren't necessarily my passion, I enjoyed working directly with museum artifacts in order to document them in PastPerfect5, the software used by Smithsonian Museums. I spent the summer in the museum's replica 1940s service station, researching and barcoding each individual artifact so that it was searchable in the museum's new digital database.

Digital Content Intern, Wavelength Marketing

This summer, I'll be working as an intern at Wavelength Marketing in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, a digital and print marketing company. I will be working directly with Wavelength's Digital Marketing Specialist to create web content for clients as well as track and report on SEO and digital media data.
